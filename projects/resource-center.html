Providing free financial education to engage users
View Project

In addition to an investment platform, Ellevest helps ladies reach their financial goals by providing supplementary financial education via the Resource Center. During the closed beta, articles promoted brand awareness, established industry credibility as a trusted source, and generated excitement/increased engagement while users waited for access to the platform. Post-beta, the Resource Center is an owned content channel that continues to grow the Ellevest mailing list.

As the design lead, I worked closely with the Chief Design Officer, Melissa Cullens, to create a modular design system for a scalable content site. I was responsible for the user experience and visual design. I also designed infographics for select articles. The Resource Center shipped in late 2015.
