After completing a Front-End Web Development course at General Assembly, I coded a prototype for New Market Goods, a socially responsible clothing line fabricated by Bangladeshi artisans. The end result was a responsive digital storefront that emulates the experience of browsing vibrant bolts of fabric at a textile market through a horizontal scrolling interaction.

I prototyped the user interactions in HTML / CSS / JavaScript; iterated on the design of the homepage and product pages based on changing business needs; and led the front-end web development and browser testing. I completed work on the prototype in 2014.
